## Key Decisions Made

**Why Hybrid GSD?**
- You need structure (planning) but also speed
- Full GSD overhead (115+ tasks, 7+ gates) is overkill
- Hybrid gives you rastreability without ceremony

**Why merge the HTMLs?**
- Single source of truth
- Consistent styling and UX
- Easier maintenance
- Professional appearance for clients

**Supabase over localStorage?**
- Real-time sync across devices/users
- Already in place (schema ready)
- Credentials configured in Vercel
- Better for team collaboration
